Priyadarshini scheme

Free travel applies to women and transgender persons on KSRTC ordinary class buses in Kerala.

Eligibility

  • Applies to KSRTC ordinary buses marked eligible by schedule data.
  • Premium classes such as AC, Swift, super fast, and deluxe are excluded.
  • Final eligibility is determined by KSRTC rules and conductor confirmation.

Data source

Schedule data is attributed to publicly available KSRTC listings from tickettogetlost.com and refreshed by a rate-limited scraper.
